General Questions

What is mentneo?

mentneo is a premier learning platform and web services company focused on providing comprehensive full-stack development training and solutions. We empower students to become skilled developers through our courses and provide businesses with high-quality web development services at affordable rates.

How can I get started with mentneo?

Getting started with mentneo is easy! You can browse our available courses on our website and enroll directly online. For personalized guidance, you can schedule a call with our team who will help you choose the right program based on your goals and background. You can also visit our course page to explore all options.

Where is mentneo located?

mentneo is headquartered in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India. However, our courses and services are available online, allowing students and clients from around the world to benefit from our offerings. We provide both remote and in-person options for many of our programs.

Do I need any prior experience to join mentneo courses?

Our courses are designed for various skill levels. While some advanced courses may require basic programming knowledge, many of our beginner-friendly programs assume no prior coding experience. Each course listing clearly indicates the prerequisites, if any. We offer preparatory materials for those who need to build foundational skills before starting more advanced programs.

Courses & Training

What courses do you offer?

mentneo offers a variety of technical courses including:

  • Full-Stack Development (MERN Stack)
  • Frontend Development with React
  • Backend Development with Node.js
  • Python Programming
  • Java Development
  • Data Science Fundamentals
  • UI/UX Design

We regularly update our curriculum to include the latest technologies and industry practices.

How long are your courses?

Our courses vary in duration depending on the content and depth. Short courses typically run for 4-6 weeks, while comprehensive bootcamps can last 12-24 weeks. We also offer self-paced options that allow you to learn according to your schedule. Each course page provides specific information about duration and time commitment.

Are the courses online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person training options. Our online courses are available worldwide, allowing you to learn from anywhere. For students in Vijayawada and surrounding areas, we also provide in-person training sessions. Some programs follow a hybrid model, combining both online and in-person elements.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes, all students who successfully complete our courses receive a certificate of completion from mentneo. These certificates validate your skills and can be shared on professional platforms like LinkedIn. For certain specialized courses, we also offer preparation for industry-recognized certifications.

Do you offer career support?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive career support including resume building, portfolio development, interview preparation, and job placement assistance. Our network of industry connections helps students find relevant opportunities after completing their courses. We maintain relationships with many tech companies and startups that regularly hire our graduates.

Services

What web development services do you provide?

Our web development services include:

  • Custom website development
  • E-commerce solutions
  • Web application development
  • API development and integration
  • Content Management Systems
  • UI/UX design
  • Website maintenance and support
  • Performance optimization

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises.

How long does it take to complete a web development project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. Simple websites may take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications could take 2-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific project needs. We always strive to deliver projects on time while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Do you provide mobile app development services?

Yes, we offer mobile app development services for both Android and iOS platforms. Our team is skilled in native development as well as cross-platform frameworks like React Native and Flutter. We can help you build apps from scratch or update existing applications to meet your business needs.

What is your development process?

Our development process follows these key steps:

  1. Discovery & Planning: Understanding your requirements and creating a roadmap
  2. Design: Creating wireframes and visual designs for approval
  3. Development: Building the core functionality and features
  4. Testing: Rigorous quality assurance and bug fixing
  5. Deployment: Launching your project on the appropriate platforms
  6. Maintenance: Ongoing support and updates as needed

We maintain transparent communication throughout each phase of the project.

Pricing & Payment

How much do your courses cost?

Our course prices vary depending on the program duration, depth of content, and level of support provided. Basic courses start at around ₹10,000, while comprehensive bootcamps range from ₹35,000 to ₹75,000. We frequently offer scholarships, early bird discounts, and flexible payment options to make our courses more accessible.

Do you offer installment payment options?

Yes, we understand that education is an investment. We offer flexible installment payment plans for most of our courses. Typically, you can divide the total fee into 2-3 installments over the course duration. For some of our premium bootcamps, we also partner with financing options that allow you to pay over a longer period.

How much do web development services cost?

Our service pricing is customized based on your specific requirements. Simple websites typically start from ₹20,000, while complex web applications and e-commerce solutions are priced according to features, scale, and complexity. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your project needs. Our pricing is competitive while ensuring quality deliverables.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit cards
  • Net banking
  • UPI transfers
  • Bank transfers/NEFT
  • Digital wallets (Paytm, Google Pay, etc.)

All our payment gateways are secure and protected.
