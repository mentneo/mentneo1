2017

Teaching Passion

Teaching isn’t just a skill for me — it’s my superpower. I love breaking down complex tech topics into simple, relatable concepts. Whether it’s HTML, CSS, React, Firebase, or full-stack development, I don’t just teach—I guide, mentor, and vibe with students like a friend who’s just a few steps ahead..

2024

Market Gap Identification

Recognized the significant gap between academic education and industry requirements. Started researching how to build a comprehensive yet affordable tech education platform focused on real-world skills.

2024

mentneo Is Born

Founded mentneo with the mission to provide affordable, high-quality education in full-stack development and related technologies. Started with a small batch of 20 students, focusing on personalized learning.

2025

Growth & Expansion

Expanded mentneo's curriculum to include specialized courses and introduced industry mentorship programs. Formed partnerships with tech companies to enhance student placement opportunities.

2025

Vision for the Future

Working on integrating AI-driven learning paths and expanding mentneo's reach globally. Focused on creating more scholarship opportunities and innovative learning methodologies.

My Vision & Philosophy

Vision

To create a world where quality technical education is accessible to everyone regardless of their background or financial constraints, enabling individuals to realize their potential and contribute to the digital economy.

Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality education with personalized mentorship, practical projects, and industry connections that prepare students for successful tech careers in an ever-evolving landscape.

Philosophy

I believe that education should be practical, engaging, and outcome-focused. Technology education must constantly evolve to stay relevant, and mentorship is as important as curriculum in student success.

Technology has the power to transform lives, but only when knowledge is accessible to all. At mentneo, we're not just teaching code—we're empowering the next generation of digital creators to build a better world.

- Abhiram Yeduru
